    IATA Professional Designation Agreement

    By clicking the "I Agree" box below, you represent and agree to the following terms, conditions and releases related to IATAs Professional Designation services:

    1)    I agree to satisfy and conduct myself in accordance with all IATA Professional Designation program policies and requirements (as they may be revised from time to time); and I shall maintain confidentiality.

     

    2)    I agree that I shall at all times act in a truthful and honest manner and provide truthful and accurate information to IATA. I agree that any intentional or unintentional failure to provide true, timely and complete responses to questions in this application may lead to further investigation and/or sanctions by IATA. I also agree to promptly report to IATA any possible violations of the terms of this Agreement or by persons who have applied for IATA Professional Designation.

     

    3)    I agree to notify IATA in a timely manner of changes concerning the information I have provided, including my current address and telephone number. Furthermore, I agree to supply any requested information or proof of payments pertaining to my application to IATA in a reasonable and timely manner. In the event of not providing this information in a reasonable and timely manner IATA may cancel my application at its discretion.

     

    4)    I have reported, and will continue to report, to the IATA Professional Designation department, within sixty (60) days of occurrence, any matters, proceedings, lawsuits, settlements and/or other agreements, administrative agency actions, or organizational actions relating to my profession or occupation, including all complaints relating to my professional activities as a professional in the aviation industry, and matters or proceedings involving, but not limited to certification, credentialing, malpractice, disciplinary ethics or similar matters. I also agree to promptly report, within sixty (60) days of occurrence, any felony criminal charges, convictions, or plea agreements or other criminal charges, convictions, or plea agreements relating to acts of dishonesty or unethical conduct.

     

    5)    I agree that if my compliance with any of the terms of this agreement requires or includes an explanation and supporting documents, I will provide a complete and accurate explanation and true copies of the materials to IATA with this application.

     

    6)    I agree that IATA has the right to communicate with any person, government agency or organization to review or confirm the information in this application or any other information related to my application for IATA credentialing. Further, I agree to and authorize the release of any information requested by IATA for such review and confirmation.

     

    7)    I agree that IATA credential status does not imply licensure, registration or government authorization to practice any specific profession or to engage in related activities.

     

    8)    I agree that all materials that I submit to IATA become the property of the IATA Professional Designation department, and that IATA is not required to return any of these materials to me.

     

    9)    I agree that upon achieving IATA credential, my name may be posted on IATAs website as part of an Online Registry to be created and maintained by IATA.

     

    10) I agree that information related to my participation in IATAs Professional Designation program may be used in an anonymous manner for research purposes only.

     

    11) I agree that all disputes relating in any way to my application for an IATA credential and/or my involvement generally in an IATA Professional Designation program, will be resolved solely and exclusively by means of IATA policies, procedures and rules.

     

    12) I accept that all fees submitted to IATA for the IATA Professional Designation Program are non-refundable.
