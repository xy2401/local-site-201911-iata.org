Fares and Ticketing training schedule

See the full list of Fares and Ticketing training courses

Please note that these courses and dates are correct as of today, but are subject to change.
January - 2020
Passenger Fares and Ticketing - Basic (Classroom, 5 days)
United Arab Emirates, Dubai (FZCO-HST) 		19 Jan - 23 Jan
  Learn the fundamentals for pricing journeys, interpreting constructed fares and decoding e-ticket data to provide your customers the best service possible.

February - 2020
Passenger Proration - Advanced (Classroom, 5 days)
Chinese Taipei, Taipei (IATA) 		3 Feb - 7 Feb

April - 2020
Repricing Exchanges and Reissues (Classroom, 4 days)
United States, Miami (IATA, ACCET Accredited) 		6 Apr - 9 Apr
  Learn to recognize ticketing entries and handle voluntary and involuntary e-ticket alterations.
Tarifas y Emisión de Billetes - Introduccion (presencial, 5 días)
United States, Miami (IATA, ACCET Accredited) 		13 Apr - 17 Apr
  Aprenda las bases para el cálculo de tarifas aéreas internacionales, el procedimiento para la construcción de la tarifa y su aplicación en los diferentes elementos que conforman el billete electrónico con objeto de proporcionar el mejor servicio a sus clientes.
Passenger Proration - Fundamentals (Classroom, 5 days)
Spain, Madrid (IATA) 		20 Apr - 24 Apr
