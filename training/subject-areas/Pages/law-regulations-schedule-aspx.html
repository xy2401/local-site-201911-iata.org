Aviation Law and Regulations training schedule

See the full list of Aviation Law and Regulations training courses

Please note that these courses and dates are correct as of today, but are subject to change
December - 2019
Aviation Law for Managers (non-lawyers) (Classroom, 5 days)
United States, Miami (IATA, ACCET Accredited) 		9 Dec - 13 Dec
  Acquire a general understanding of aviation law relevant to air navigation services, airlines, civil aviation authorities and airports.
Legal and Financial Issues for Air Navigation Service Providers (Classroom, 5 days)
Switzerland, Geneva (IATA) 		9 Dec - 13 Dec
  Understand the impact of state rights and obligations, charging policy, facilities financing, management and control on your air navigation services.
Cargo Claims and Complaint Handling (Classroom, 2 days)
China, Beijing (IATA) 		9 Dec - 10 Dec
  This course will provide you with the knowledge of the current crucial legal aspects of carriage of cargo by air for all industry stakeholders who are concerned with effective cargo claims handling and loss prevention.
Aircraft Airworthiness and Air Carrier Certification (Classroom, 5 days)
Turkey, Istanbul (TKAA-RTP) 		9 Dec - 13 Dec
  Gain the knowledge and skills to manage aircraft certification and continuing airworthiness as per the current ICAO Annexes, regulations and requirements (FAA, JAA, EASA).
Aviation Law and Regulations (Classroom, 5 days)
Hong Kong SAR, Hong Kong (JAA-RTP) 		9 Dec - 13 Dec
  An overview of all relevant aspects of aviation law and regulation required in the airline industry and its partners/counterparts
Civil Aviation Management (Classroom, 5 days)
Thailand, Bangkok (BAA-RTP) 		16 Dec - 20 Dec
  Learn the essentials of civil aviation management.
Aircraft Acquisition and Financing (Classroom, 4 days)
Singapore, Singapore (IATA) 		17 Dec - 20 Dec
  Financial and legal fundamentals to help you choose, draft, and negotiate an aircraft acquisition agreement.
