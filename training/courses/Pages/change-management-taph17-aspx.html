    Change Management (Classroom, 3 days)


    DescriptionPrices & Registration
    Did you know that in the past 3 years, 111 participants from 22 countries have taken this course with IATA?

    About

    Learn how to effectively lead and manage important organizational changes through proven methodology and experiential exercises.


    This course is available at IATA Training Centers, Regional Training Partner locations, and on-demand as in-company training.

    Course format

    • This classroom course provides 3 days (24 hours) of instruction delivered by an official IATA Instructor.
    • Student performance will be based on an assessment

    Prerequisites

    • Participants are required to complete a questionnaire prior to the course describing their change project or issue, its impact and status
    • Recommended level is Entry-level and Intermediate
    • The recommended level of language proficiency is ICAO Operational Level 4 for courses in English or equivalent for other languages.

    Next class

    Feb 19 - Feb 21, 2020 - Switzerland, Geneva (IATA)
    3 additional classes are scheduled. See all

    What you will learn

    On completion of this course you will be able to:

    • Develop a change strategy focusing on the people side of change
    • Work with a simulation to identify key issues, create and implement a change plan
    • Create buy-in and readiness for change
    • Develop a personalized change leadership development plan
    • Obtain coaching on your change leadership style and strategy

    Course content

    • Characteristics of effective change teams
    • Personalized leadership assessment
    • Coaching and personalized development plans
    • Building a case for change
    • Leveraging resistance to change

    Who should attend

    This course is recommended for:

    • Directors
    • Managers
    • HR managers

    Certificate awarded

    An IATA Certificate of Completion is awarded to participants obtaining a grade of 70% or higher on all exercises and exams. A special distinction is awarded to participants obtaining a grade of 90% or higher.

    This course is a step toward earning an IATA Diploma in:

    • Human Resources Management Diploma
    • General Management Diploma
