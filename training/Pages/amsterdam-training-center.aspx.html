    IATA Training Center in Amsterdam, Netherlands

    All training courses at our Amsterdam Training Center are tax exempt from June 1, 2017 onwards! Take advantage of the savings you can make by booking your seat today!  IATA’s Amsterdam training center is located in the Airport Business Park Lijnden on the ground floor of  the IATA office.

    Amsterdam course schedule
    December - 2019
    IGOM Implementation and Standardized Procedures (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2019)     		4 Dec - 6 Dec
      Learn the scope, applicability and benefits of the IATA Ground Operations Manual (IGOM)
    Airline Leading Practices and Cost Reduction Strategies (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2019)     		9 Dec - 13 Dec
      Review the leading practices of successful airlines from both the low cost and network carrier sectors. Learn procedures to maintain a viable cost reduction program within your organization.
    Route Forecasting and Development (Classroom, 4 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2019)     		17 Dec - 20 Dec
      Learn methods for calculating the financial performance of different routes, forecasting cost and revenue drivers, and planning for the future development of your network.

    January - 2020
    Airline Business Models and Competitive Strategies (Classroom, 5 days) Virtual Simulation Program
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2019)     		27 Jan - 31 Jan
      Test your management skills and strategic thinking to run your own airline and decide on the network, fleet, schedule, marketing and revenue management.

    February - 2020
    Aircraft Weight & Balance Refresher (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2019)     		5 Feb - 7 Feb
      Refresh your formal knowledge on theories and principles of weight and balance as per the standard IATA Design Manual and Automated Load Sheet.
    Airline Business Foundations (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		10 Feb - 14 Feb
      If you’re a new airline recruit or an aviation company with airline clients, this course will bring you up to speed on key airline business practices to help you succeed in the industry.
    Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) - Initial - Category 6 (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		17 Feb - 21 Feb
      Gain the competencies to accept, handle and process shipments containing dangerous goods according to the current edition of the IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) manual.
    Audit, Quality and Risk Management for Temperature Controlled Cargo (Classroom, 5 Days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		24 Feb - 28 Feb
      This course provides a standard internal audit process you can immediately apply within your business to ensure compliance with common regulations and quality standards.

    March - 2020
    Revenue Management and Pricing with simulation (Classroom, 4 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		10 Mar - 13 Mar
      Explore the fundamentals and principles of airline revenue management (RM) through a combination of theory and hands-on practice, and validate your learning using our sophisticated airline revenue management simulator.
    Introduction to Flight Operations Management (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		16 Mar - 20 Mar
      Align your flight operations with regulatory requirements and learn how to manage recruitment and training.
    Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) - Recurrent - Category 6 (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		25 Mar - 27 Mar
      Revalidate your 24-month IATA Dangerous Goods (DGR) requirement and stay up-to-date with the legal requirements, operational restrictions, and documentation regulations for dangerous goods.
    Security Management Systems - SeMS (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		30 Mar - 3 Apr
      Learn the essentials of IATA's SeMS initiative and how it can make your organization IOSA compliant. Leave the course with tools to build a SeMS that produces measurable and auditable results.

    April - 2020
    Law of Aviation Insurance (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		6 Apr - 8 Apr
      Gain clarity on the main aviation insurance conventions and protocols, types of coverage available, and practices for insuring hull and liability risks.
    Live Animals Cargo Logistics Management (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		15 Apr - 17 Apr
      Learn the best practices and standards for the carriage of live animals by air
    Network, Fleet and Schedule Planning (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		20 Apr - 24 Apr
      Learn key route forecasting and scheduling techniques that will help you get the best use of your aircraft resources and fleet.
    Ancillary Revenue Strategies (Classroom, 4 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		28 Apr - 1 May
      Learn how ancillary sales work, the impact on airline and distribution systems, and how to develop ancillary revenue.

    May - 2020
    Crew Resource Management (CRM) Implementation (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		6 May - 8 May
      Strategies to optimize your use of staff, equipment and procedures to prevent error at each phase of flight.
    Crew Resource Management for Instructors (CRMI) - (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		11 May - 13 May
      This train-the-trainer course lets you practice teaching techniques that will make your CRM training effective and motivating for your students.
    Instructional Techniques (Classroom)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		18 May - 20 May
      This course will develop your knowledge, attitudes, and skills required to effectively develop, conduct and evaluate a course.
    Repricing Exchanges and Reissues (Classroom, 4 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		26 May - 29 May
      Learn to recognize ticketing entries and handle voluntary and involuntary e-ticket alterations.

    June - 2020
    Airport Slots and Coordination (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		3 Jun - 5 Jun
      Learn how to apply the standard communication procedures from IATA's Standard Schedules Information Manual (SSIM) to messages between airlines and slot coordinators.
    Procurement and Contracts Management (30 PDUs, classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		8 Jun - 12 Jun
      Learn how to properly manage and control the procurement process with your suppliers.
    Air Cargo Management (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		15 Jun - 19 Jun
      Review the cargo system with an international perspective, focusing on the business, operations and regulatory fundamentals.
    Security Audit and Quality Control (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		22 Jun - 26 Jun
      Ensure your compliance with ICAO Annex 17 audit requirements, plus international and national security programs. Learn how to conduct audits and quality control across multiple security segments.

    July - 2020
    Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) – Shippers and Packers - Category 1, 2 (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		1 Jul - 3 Jul
      Knowledge and skills to pack and ship shipments containing dangerous goods as per the current edition of the IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations Manual.
    Temperature Controlled Container Operations (Classroom, 2 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		6 Jul - 7 Jul
      This course will bring you up-to-speed on container types and their special operating procedures so you can ensure your equipment and services meet industry requirements.
    Temperature Controlled Cargo Operations (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		8 Jul - 10 Jul
      Learn the requirements of the healthcare industry for an air cargo logistics service provider, to become a player in the pharmaceutical logistics market.
    Transport of Dangerous Goods by Sea (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		15 Jul - 17 Jul
      Understand the IMDG Code regulations and requirements for classification, packaging, marking, labeling, documentation, container and vehicle packing, and vessel stowage.
    Sales Accounting and Control (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		20 Jul - 24 Jul
      Achieve maximum accuracy in your passenger sales accounting activities.

    August - 2020
    Unruly Passenger Prevention and Response (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		5 Aug - 7 Aug
      Learn how to identify and calm unruly behavior in its early stages; develop your skills with role-play exercises carried out throughout the course.
    CEIV Pharma Refresher course for Key Personnel (Classroom, 2 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		17 Aug - 18 Aug
      Quality and risk management systems in the transport, handling, and manipulation of Pharma time and temperature-sensitive shipments
    Airline Sales Strategies (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		19 Aug - 21 Aug
      Learn new techniques to manage and negotiate complex sales transactions: identifying strategic sales targets, anticipating customer responses and consultative selling techniques.
    Benchmarking and Best Practices for Air Navigation Service Providers (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		24 Aug - 28 Aug
      Learn how to use benchmarking effectively to deliver service improvements and achieve better performance results.
    Air Cargo Security (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		31 Aug - 4 Sep
      Threat assessment and planning techniques to help your business comply with recent changes to security regulations. Learn to apply common industry practices using hands-on simulation exercises.

    September - 2020
    Managing Air Cargo Operations (Classroom, 4 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		8 Sep - 11 Sep
      Learn effective techniques of managing Air Cargo Operations in order to save money, avoid risks, and enhance operations.
    Professional Skills for DGR Instructors - Categories 1,2,3,6 (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		14 Sep - 18 Sep
      Confidently apply instructional design principles and instructional techniques to design and deliver an IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations training program.
    Live Animals Transportation (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		23 Sep - 25 Sep
      Learn the most up-to-date airline and government requirements pertaining to the transport of live animals by air.
    Behavioral Analysis and Predictive Screening Strategies (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		28 Sep - 2 Oct
      Learn passenger security screening techniques that increase efficiency and throughput, including: questioning techniques and how to detect suspicious signs before making contact with a passenger.

    October - 2020
    Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) - Initial - Category 6 (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		5 Oct - 9 Oct
      Gain the competencies to accept, handle and process shipments containing dangerous goods according to the current edition of the IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) manual.
    Aviation Security Train the Trainer (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		12 Oct - 16 Oct
      Learn how to design lesson plans and instructional aids based on performance objectives. Gain practical experience by preparing and presenting short lessons during class.
    Perishable Cargo Logistics Management (Classroom, 3 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		21 Oct - 23 Oct
      Learn how to use IATA’s Perishable Cargo Regulations (PCR), explore the HACCP methodology and learn how to use IATA’s CEIV fresh audit checklist to implement it in your organization
    Airline Leading Practices and Cost Reduction Strategies (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		26 Oct - 30 Oct
      Review the leading practices of successful airlines from both the low cost and network carrier sectors. Learn procedures to maintain a viable cost reduction program within your organization.

    November - 2020
    Document Control Systems (Classroom, 4 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		3 Nov - 6 Nov
      This hands-on course gives you a test environment to build your own document control procedures, including: process mapping, referencing and amendments.
    Management of Training (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		9 Nov - 13 Nov
      Position training at the heart of organizational growth and improvement.
    Airport Marketing & Corporate Communications Strategies (Classroom, 5 days)
    Netherlands, Amsterdam (IATA-2021)     		16 Nov - 20 Nov
      Tools to maximize profit within your catchment area by attracting retailers and service providers. Learn to customize your marketing campaigns to bring in new airlines and higher flight frequencies.
    Airport Strategic Management with Business Simulation (Classroom, 10 days)
