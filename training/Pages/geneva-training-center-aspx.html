    IATA Training Center in Geneva, Switzerland

    The Geneva training center hosts 10 state-of-the-art classrooms looking over the Geneva airport and Jura mountains. Our premises are ideally located next to the airport (less than a three-minute walk) and within six minutes of the city center by train. The IATA Geneva team looks forward to making your stay as pleasant as possible. Feel free to contact us before or during your stay in Geneva!
