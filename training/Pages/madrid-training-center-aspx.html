    IATA Training Center in Madrid, Spain

    ​The Madrid training center is located in the IATA offices at Paseo de la Castellana, one of the city's most important arteries, and opposite the Santiago Bernabéu football Stadium, home to Real Madrid. It takes only ten minutes to get to the historic city center.

    Madrid course schedule
    January - 2020
    Airport Slots and Coordination (Classroom, 3 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		15 Jan - 17 Jan
      Learn how to apply the standard communication procedures from IATA's Standard Schedules Information Manual (SSIM) to messages between airlines and slot coordinators.

    February - 2020
    Temperature Controlled Cargo Operations (Classroom, 3 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		5 Feb - 7 Feb
      Learn the requirements of the healthcare industry for an air cargo logistics service provider, to become a player in the pharmaceutical logistics market.

    March - 2020
    European Union (EU) Aviation Law (Classroom, 3 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		4 Mar - 6 Mar
      Gain up-to-date knowledge of the challenges faced by airlines operating to, from or within the European Union (EU).
    Station / Ground Handling Management (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		23 Mar - 27 Mar
      Examine the functions and management requirements of Ground Handling in the passenger and cargo terminal and the ramp.
    Airline Business Models and Competitive Strategies (Classroom, 5 days) Virtual Simulation Program
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		30 Mar - 3 Apr
      Test your management skills and strategic thinking to run your own airline and decide on the network, fleet, schedule, marketing and revenue management.

    April - 2020
    Perishable Cargo Logistics Management (Classroom, 3 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		6 Apr - 8 Apr
      Learn how to use IATA’s Perishable Cargo Regulations (PCR), explore the HACCP methodology and learn how to use IATA’s CEIV fresh audit checklist to implement it in your organization
    International Air Law for Lawyers and Legal Professionals (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		13 Apr - 17 Apr
      Strengthen your understanding of air service agreements and public and international air law.
    Passenger Proration - Fundamentals (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		20 Apr - 24 Apr
      Apply the rules of the Prorate Manual-Passenger (PMP) and the Multilateral Proration Agreement-Passenger (MPA-P) within your organization.
    Aviation Fuel Management Essentials (Classroom, 3 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		27 Apr - 29 Apr
      Follow the supply chain from refinery to delivery into the aircraft. Learn how to minimize fuel uplift and how fuel suppliers ensure the safe delivery of fuel.

    May - 2020
    Introduction to Airline Distribution and e-Commerce (Classroom, 3 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		11 May - 13 May
      Learn the fundamentals of airline distribution and e-commerce and how this plays a role in distribution management for an airline.
    Cualificaciones Profesionales para Instructores en DGR - Categorías 1, 2, 3, 6 (presencial, 5 días)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		18 May - 22 May
      Aprenda a aplicar el principio de la practica sobre los programas de capacitación en normativas relativas a mercancías peligrosas.
    Security Management Systems - SeMS (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		25 May - 29 May
      Learn the essentials of IATA's SeMS initiative and how it can make your organization IOSA compliant. Leave the course with tools to build a SeMS that produces measurable and auditable results.

    June - 2020
    Digital Airline and Change Strategy (Classroom, 3 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		3 Jun - 5 Jun
      Learn about the major digital transformation initiatives impacting the airline and its finance function, and build a change strategy with the help of IATA experts.
    Transporte de baterías de litio por vía aérea (presencial, 2 días)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		8 Jun - 9 Jun
      Adquirir las habilidades y conocimientos necesarios para embalar y transportar las pilas y baterías de ión de litio y de metal de litio por vía aérea
    Curso de entrenamiento para capacitadores (presencial, 3 días)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		10 Jun - 12 Jun
      Este curso le ayudará a desarrollar sus competencias como docente, al ofrecer múltiples oportunidades de valorar y de mejorar su trabajo a la hora de plantear, desarrollar y analizar un evento formativo.
    Airline Distribution Strategies and e-Commerce (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		15 Jun - 19 Jun
      Take an inside look at today's leading distribution models and strategies, gaining tools to optimize your company’s distribution processes.
    Airline Financial Risk Management (Classroom, 4 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		23 Jun - 26 Jun
      Gain a deeper understanding of the eight economic and financial risks to which airlines are exposed and how each risk affects the business in terms of the direction and the size of the effect.
    Human Factors in Aviation (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		29 Jun - 3 Jul
      Gain a comprehensive overview of the effect and management of human factors in aviation with this foundation course.

    July - 2020
    Network, Fleet and Schedule Planning (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		6 Jul - 10 Jul
      Learn key route forecasting and scheduling techniques that will help you get the best use of your aircraft resources and fleet.
    Cargo Skills and Procedures (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		13 Jul - 17 Jul
      Develop a solid foundation for your cargo career by learning the fundamentals of cargo skills and procedures.
    Monitoring and Controlling Multiple Projects, 30 PDUs (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		27 Jul - 31 Jul
      This course will equip you with tools and methodology you can easily apply when taking on multiple projects, programs, and portfolios.

    August - 2020
    International Negotiation Skills (Classroom)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		5 Aug - 7 Aug
      Prepare for each phase of a negotiation process, and determine both short-term and long-term costs of negotiating.
    Advanced Safety Management Systems (SMS) in Civil Aviation (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		31 Aug - 4 Sep
      Build on your knowledge of SMS, examining hazard and risk management, SMS operation, safety assurance and strategic integration.

    September - 2020
    Security Checkpoint Management (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		7 Sep - 11 Sep
      Evaluate the capabilities and limitations of your technology and team against the IATA-ACI Security and Egress Project criteria to increase efficiency in your screening operations.
    Cargo Revenue Management with Optimization Simulations (Classroom, 4 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		22 Sep - 25 Sep
      Manage sales, pricing, inventory and systems to capture revenue opportunities and remain competitive.
    New Distribution Capability Readiness (Classroom)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		30 Sep - 2 Oct
      A step-by-step approach to show how your airline can assess business and technical NDC readiness.

    October - 2020
    Integrated Approach to Passenger Revenue Accounting and Control (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		5 Oct - 9 Oct
      Protect your revenue and increase productivity with a complete review of passenger revenue accounting and control activities.
    Revenue Management and Pricing with simulation (Classroom, 4 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		13 Oct - 16 Oct
      Explore the fundamentals and principles of airline revenue management (RM) through a combination of theory and hands-on practice, and validate your learning using our sophisticated airline revenue management simulator.
    Management of Aviation Facilitation (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		26 Oct - 30 Oct
      Review the hot topics of airport access control, harmonized regulations and perimeter security with the aim of providing a seamless end-to-end passenger experience.

    November - 2020
    Carbon Markets, Low Carbon and Sustainable Alternative Fuels (Classroom, 2 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		4 Nov - 5 Nov
      Learn the fundamentals of carbon markets and sustainable alternative fuels (SAF) and their essential roles in the future of aviation.
    Airport Slots and Coordination (Classroom, 3 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		11 Nov - 13 Nov
      Learn how to apply the standard communication procedures from IATA's Standard Schedules Information Manual (SSIM) to messages between airlines and slot coordinators.
    Airline Contract Law (Classroom, 4 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		17 Nov - 20 Nov
      Learn the basic drafting principles and agreements for aircraft sales and purchases, interlining, maintenance and catering, ground handling and more.
    International Air Law for Lawyers and Legal Professionals (Classroom, 5 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		23 Nov - 27 Nov
      Strengthen your understanding of air service agreements and public and international air law.

    December - 2020
    Passenger Assessment and Travel Document Checks (Classroom, 3 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		9 Dec - 11 Dec
      Learn about machine-readable passports and ID cards, false documents, techniques for passenger assessment and exception handling.
    Airline Marketing (Classroom, 4 days)
    Spain, Madrid (IATA)     		15 Dec - 18 Dec
      Marketing concepts for customer acquisition and retention, including: commercial strategy development, product management, market research techniques and distribution channels.
