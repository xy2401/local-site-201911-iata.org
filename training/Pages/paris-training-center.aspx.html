    IATA Training Center in Paris, France

    Our offices are located in Boulogne Billancourt, a city within easy reach of Paris. We are also located directly above a metro line that takes you directly to the Eiffel Tower, the Champs Elysees and the famous shops near Opera Garnier. The Paris team looks forward to welcoming you and making your stay as pleasant as possible.

    Paris course schedule
    December - 2019
    Re-imagining Passenger Experience Through Design Thinking (Classroom, 2 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		3 Dec - 4 Dec
      An introduction to the design thinking process, and how to apply 'human-centered' design techniques, in combination with analytical research of customers
    Business Aviation Handling and Fixed-Base Operations, FBO (Classroom, 3 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		17 Dec - 19 Dec
      Learn best practices in commercial airline ground operations to build a profitable business model.

    February - 2020
    Airport Terminal Planning and Design (Classroom, 5 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		10 Feb - 14 Feb
      Explore the principles of terminal design, optimal flows and capacity. Learn how infrastructure design can support your terminal operations.
    Air Transport Economics (Classroom, 5 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		24 Feb - 28 Feb
      Leveraging IATA know-how look deeper into economic and policy developments affecting the global air transport industry. Examine Financial Forecasts, Industry Outlooks, profitability, regulations.

    March - 2020
    Airport Extreme Weather Operations and Planning (Classroom, 4 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		10 Mar - 13 Mar
      Explore planning tools and procedures to help your airport respond to adverse conditions. Learn how to establish a proactive culture across your operations and organization.
    Aviation and the Environment (Classroom, 4 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		24 Mar - 27 Mar
      Be up-to-date on aviation environmental issues and their management, with a special emphasis on new technology, improved operations and modernized methods of airspace and airport management.
    Managing Accident Prevention and Investigation (Classroom, 5 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		30 Mar - 3 Apr
      Learn aviation incident and accident investigation requirements, techniques and working practices. Acquire a structured approach to managing safety to prevent incidents and accidents.

    April - 2020
    Revenue Management for Practitioners with simulation (Classroom, 4 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		14 Apr - 17 Apr
      Gain a competitive advantage with this advanced course on airline revenue management (RM) concepts, and validate your learning using our sophisticated airline management revenue simulator.
    Crisis Communications and Media Response for Airport and Station Managers (Classroom, 2 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		27 Apr - 28 Apr
      Practical communication skills to manage media response in an emergency and crisis situation before the corporate media management response team takes over.
    Aircraft Decommissioning Workshop (Classroom, 2 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		29 Apr - 30 Apr
      Business processes, operational experiences and best practices to optimize the residual value from aircraft decommissioning

    May - 2020
    Comptences professionnelles pour instructeurs en DGR - catgories 1, 2, 3, 6 (en salle de cours, 5 jours) - Classroom - Franais
    France, Paris (IATA)     		11 May - 15 May
      Les participants apprendront et pratiqueront une palette de techniques fondamentales pour diriger l'apprentissage destin aux adultes, concevoir des programmes de formation et utiliser des techniques de facilitation.
    Global Aviation Competition/Antitrust Law (Classroom, 3 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		18 May - 20 May
      Gain a clear understanding of the relevant practical issues of EU competition substantive rules, as well as EU institutions and their rules.
    Aeronautical Information Management (AIM) (Classroom, 5 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		25 May - 29 May
      Stay up-to-date with the latest in Aeronautical Information Management (AIM), reviewing SWIM, eTOD, AICM/ AIXM, aerodrome mapping, aeronautical information briefing, e-AIP, AIS/MET datalink.

    June - 2020
    Project Management Essentials (30 PDUs, classroom, 5 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		1 Jun - 5 Jun
      This course will teach you the fundamentals of project management.
    ISAGO for Auditors (Classroom, 4 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		9 Jun - 12 Jun
      A requirement for all ISAGO Pool member auditors, this course covers the audit process specific to ISAGO as well as its standards and recommended practices.
    European Union (EU) Aviation Law (Classroom, 3 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		17 Jun - 19 Jun
      Gain up-to-date knowledge of the challenges faced by airlines operating to, from or within the European Union (EU).
    Performance-Based Navigation (PBN) I (Classroom, 5 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		22 Jun - 26 Jun
      Gain an overview of the history of the PBN concept, GNSS theory, Area Navigation (RNAV), RNP, and airspace planning and design.
    Understanding NDC Architecture (Classroom, 2 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		29 Jun - 30 Jun
      An introduction to NDC with terminology, concepts, API schemas for shopping and order management, and airline reference architecture

    July - 2020
    Effective Communication Skills (Classroom)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		1 Jul - 3 Jul
      Improve your communication and presentation skills in a multicultural environment.
    Security Management Systems - SeMS (Classroom, 5 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		6 Jul - 10 Jul
      Learn the essentials of IATA's SeMS initiative and how it can make your organization IOSA compliant. Leave the course with tools to build a SeMS that produces measurable and auditable results.

    August - 2020
    Passenger Proration - Advanced (Classroom, 5 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		31 Aug - 4 Sep
      Assess your entitlement in accordance with the Multilateral Proration Agreements-Passenger (MPA-P).

    September - 2020
    Aviation Security Train the Trainer (Classroom, 5 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		7 Sep - 11 Sep
      Learn how to design lesson plans and instructional aids based on performance objectives. Gain practical experience by preparing and presenting short lessons during class.
    Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) - Instructor Refresher (Classroom, 3 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		16 Sep - 18 Sep
      Stay up-to-date with your knowledge of the IATA/ICAO Dangerous Goods Regulations.
    Rglements de l'IATA relatifs aux matires dangereuses (DGR) - Catgorie initiale 6 (en salle de cours, 5 jours) - Classroom - Franais
    France, Paris (IATA)     		21 Sep - 25 Sep
      Obtenez les comptences ncessaires pour accepter, manipuler et traiter des cargaisons contenant des matires dangereuses, conformment l'dition actuelle du manuel de l'IATA sur les rglements relatifs aux matires dangereuses (DGR).
    Business Aviation Handling and Fixed-Base Operations, FBO (Classroom, 3 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		30 Sep - 2 Oct
      Learn best practices in commercial airline ground operations to build a profitable business model.

    October - 2020
    Project Management - Advanced (18 PDUs, classroom)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		5 Oct - 7 Oct
      Further develop your project planning skills and acquire practical techniques to deal with real projects.
    ISAGO for Ground Service Providers (Classroom)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		13 Oct - 16 Oct
      This workshop will teach you how to apply the tools and skills of the ISAGO Program to prepare your organization for a successful audit.
    Infectious Substances Transport (Classroom, 3 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		21 Oct - 23 Oct
      In accordance with Division 6.2 Category A and B requirements, this course will keep you compliant with regulations for shipping infectious substances, such as those used in medical research and diagnostic fields.
    Digital Airline and Change Strategy (Classroom, 3 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		28 Oct - 30 Oct
      Learn about the major digital transformation initiatives impacting the airline and its finance function, and build a change strategy with the help of IATA experts.

    November - 2020
    Airport Financial Management (Classroom, 5 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		2 Nov - 6 Nov
      Learn the essentials to manage a profitable airport: how to assess financial performance, control costs and analyze the impact of industry financial trends on airport strategy and operations.
    Low Cost Carrier (LCC) Business Models (Classroom, 3 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		18 Nov - 20 Nov
      Become a Low Cost Carrier (LCC) specialist with comprehensive knowledge to establish or manage a successful and sustainable LCC.
    Airlines Understanding and Adopting NDC (Classroom, 3.5 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		24 Nov - 27 Nov
      An in depth review of New Distribution Capability (NDC) for airlines to build your business case and deployment options for your distribution strategy.
    State Safety Program (SSP) (Classroom, 5 days)
    France, Paris (IATA)     		30 Nov - 4 Dec
      Learn how to develop, implement and maintain a State Safety Program (SSP) in compliance with the relevant ICAO Standards and Recommended Practices (SARPs),
