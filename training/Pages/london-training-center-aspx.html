    London Training Center

    The IATA London Training Center is hosted by the Imparando City of London Learning Center, which is easily accessible by all forms of public transport. It is only 25 minutes from London City airport by train and underground (tube) or 60 minutes from Heathrow. It is walking distance from the Aldgate East tube station which connects to the District and Hammersmith & City lines. Hotels, restaurants, and shops are within walking distance.
