    Hong Kong SAR course schedule
    January - 2020
    Shipping Lithium Batteries by Air (Classroom, 2 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		9 Jan - 10 Jan
      Acquire the skills and knowledge to pack and ship lithium batteries by air, in compliance with the ICAO Technical Instruction and IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR).

    February - 2020
    Human Factors in Ground Operations (Classroom, 3 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		3 Feb - 5 Feb
      Learn how to improve safety and effectiveness in your ground operations with these recommendations for reducing human error.
    Airport Marketing & Corporate Communications Strategies (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		10 Feb - 14 Feb
      Tools to maximize profit within your catchment area by attracting retailers and service providers. Learn to customize your marketing campaigns to bring in new airlines and higher flight frequencies.
    Civil Aviation Management (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		17 Feb - 21 Feb
      Learn the essentials of civil aviation management.
    Senior Civil Aviation Management (Classroom)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		24 Feb - 28 Feb
      Learn how to execute the delivery of sustainable civil aviation regulatory and operational services, to meet national air transportation policies and objectives.

    March - 2020
    Aviation Cyber Security (Classroom, 3 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		11 Mar - 13 Mar
      This course will help your organization determine where to start in what can seem like a never-ending effort to protect your information and systems.
    Temperature Controlled Cargo Operations (Classroom, 3 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		16 Mar - 18 Mar
      Learn the requirements of the healthcare industry for an air cargo logistics service provider, to become a player in the pharmaceutical logistics market.
    Professional Skills for DGR Instructors - Categories 1,2,3,6 (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		30 Mar - 3 Apr
      Confidently apply instructional design principles and instructional techniques to design and deliver an IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations training program.

    April - 2020
    Revenue Management and Pricing with simulation (Classroom, 4 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		14 Apr - 17 Apr
      Explore the fundamentals and principles of airline revenue management (RM) through a combination of theory and hands-on practice, and validate your learning using our sophisticated airline revenue management simulator.
    Instructional Design (Classroom)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		20 Apr - 22 Apr
      Systematically design, develop, implement, and evaluate training using an instructional design model.

    May - 2020
    Network, Fleet and Schedule Planning (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		11 May - 15 May
      Learn key route forecasting and scheduling techniques that will help you get the best use of your aircraft resources and fleet.
    Airport Certification and Infrastructure Standards (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		18 May - 22 May
      Review aerodrome operator obligations based on ICAO Annex 14 in order to assess your facility’s level of compliance. Learn how to recommend structural changes and improve day-to-day operations.

    June - 2020
    Aviation Internal Auditor (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		8 Jun - 12 Jun
      This course prepares you to conduct consistent audits and drive continuous improvement within your organization.
    Airport Development and Infrastructure Design (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		15 Jun - 19 Jun
      Learn IATA best practices and key principles from the Airport Development Reference Manual (ADRM) to plan and expand airport infrastructure.
    Train the Trainer (Classroom, 3 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		22 Jun - 24 Jun
      Build on your existing instructional skills, giving you multiple opportunities to evaluate and improve the way you design, conduct, and evaluate a training event.

    July - 2020
    Instructional Techniques for DGR - Categories 4, 5, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12 (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		6 Jul - 10 Jul
      Learn the techniques and strategies to plan a comprehensive dangerous goods training program
    AVSEC Background Vetting and Insider Threat Mitigation (Classroom, 3 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		22 Jul - 24 Jul
      Based on recommendations from the ICAO Universal Security Audit Program and the IATA Security Manual, this course helps you develop an employee background vetting program that fits the context of your company.

    August - 2020
    Air Cargo Security (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		10 Aug - 14 Aug
      Threat assessment and planning techniques to help your business comply with recent changes to security regulations. Learn to apply common industry practices using hands-on simulation exercises.
    Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) - Initial - Category 6 (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		17 Aug - 21 Aug
      Gain the competencies to accept, handle and process shipments containing dangerous goods according to the current edition of the IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) manual.
    Instructional Techniques (Classroom)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		26 Aug - 28 Aug
      This course will develop your knowledge, attitudes, and skills required to effectively develop, conduct and evaluate a course.
    Passenger Data Exchange Programs (API/PNR) (Classroom, 3 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		31 Aug - 2 Sep
      Working from the API-PNR Toolkit, the practical exercises in this course will help you understand the steps to building a data program in alignment with international standards.

    September - 2020
    Airline Business Models and Competitive Strategies (Classroom, 5 days) Virtual Simulation Program
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		7 Sep - 11 Sep
      Test your management skills and strategic thinking to run your own airline and decide on the network, fleet, schedule, marketing and revenue management.
    SGHA-SLA and Effective Negotiation Behaviors (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		14 Sep - 18 Sep
      Learn how to use the latest version of the SGHA to its fullest as a commercial agreement and services description document.
    Cargo Warehouse Operations and ULD Build-up (Classroom, 4 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		22 Sep - 25 Sep
      Gain the tools and techniques needed to ensure that your warehouse processes are safe, efficient and cost effective.
    Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) - Instructor Refresher (Classroom, 3 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		28 Sep - 30 Sep
      Stay up-to-date with your knowledge of the IATA/ICAO Dangerous Goods Regulations.

    October - 2020
    Baggage Handling Services and Systems (Classroom, 3 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		7 Oct - 9 Oct
      Learn how to evaluate the complex baggage handling system, from check-in to baggage claim, with the aim of maximizing efficiency and enhancing customer service.
    Security Management Systems - SeMS (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		12 Oct - 16 Oct
      Learn the essentials of IATA's SeMS initiative and how it can make your organization IOSA compliant. Leave the course with tools to build a SeMS that produces measurable and auditable results.
    Professional Skills for DGR Instructors - Categories 1,2,3,6 (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		19 Oct - 23 Oct
      Confidently apply instructional design principles and instructional techniques to design and deliver an IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations training program.
    Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) - Recurrent - Category 6 (Classroom, 3 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		28 Oct - 30 Oct
      Revalidate your 24-month IATA Dangerous Goods (DGR) requirement and stay up-to-date with the legal requirements, operational restrictions, and documentation regulations for dangerous goods.

    November - 2020
    Data Protection – Key Principles and International Framework (Classroom, 2 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		2 Nov - 3 Nov
      Identify solutions to keep your clients happy, their data safe and avoid sanctions
    Safety Management Systems (SMS) - Train the Trainer (Classroom, 3 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		4 Nov - 6 Nov
      Learn how to train to improve performance, introduce safer operations, and reduce costs in your airline while ensuring compliance with ICAO SMS requirements.
    Legal and Financial Issues for Air Navigation Service Providers (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		9 Nov - 13 Nov
      Understand the impact of state rights and obligations, charging policy, facilities financing, management and control on your air navigation services.
    Aviation Security Train the Trainer (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		16 Nov - 20 Nov
      Learn how to design lesson plans and instructional aids based on performance objectives. Gain practical experience by preparing and presenting short lessons during class.
    Lean - Six Sigma in Aviation (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		23 Nov - 27 Nov
      This course will provide you with an overview of the lean methodology and the tools necessary to support improvement projects and the application of Lean Six Sigma in your company.
    Audit, Quality and Risk Management for Temperature Controlled Cargo (Classroom, 5 Days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		30 Nov - 4 Dec
      This course provides a standard internal audit process you can immediately apply within your business to ensure compliance with common regulations and quality standards.

    December - 2020
    Airport Total Quality Management and Customer Service (Classroom, 5 days)
    Hong Kong SAR, China (IATA)     		7 Dec - 11 Dec
      Learn how to develop a TQM strategy and quality culture that achieves organizational effectiveness across all airport operations.
