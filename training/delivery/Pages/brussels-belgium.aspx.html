    IATA Training Center in Brussels, Belgium

    The Brussels Training Center is conveniently situated in the center of Brussels, located in the city's shopping area and in close proximity to Bois de la Cambre the Place du Chatelain.

    Brussels course schedule
    February - 2020
    Air Cargo Security (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		3 Feb - 7 Feb
      Threat assessment and planning techniques to help your business comply with recent changes to security regulations. Learn to apply common industry practices using hands-on simulation exercises.
    Human Factors in Aviation (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		17 Feb - 21 Feb
      Gain a comprehensive overview of the effect and management of human factors in aviation with this foundation course.

    March - 2020
    Passenger Data Exchange Programs (API/PNR) (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		11 Mar - 13 Mar
      Working from the API-PNR Toolkit, the practical exercises in this course will help you understand the steps to building a data program in alignment with international standards.

    April - 2020
    Aviation Lead Auditor (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		1 Apr - 3 Apr
      Gain the essential knowledge required to become a lead auditor by further developing your practical audit management and team leadership skills.
    Security Risk and Crisis Management (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		6 Apr - 10 Apr
      Address security vulnerabilities in your organization and equip your facility to handle the aftermath of a crisis. Learn how to assess risks, develop a crisis plan and set up a crisis center.
    Safety Management Systems (SMS) - Implementation and Control (Classroom)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		14 Apr - 17 Apr
      This practical course gives you the expertise to implement a successful Safety Management System (SMS) in your airline with the latest tools and processes.
    Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) - Initial - Category 6 (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		20 Apr - 24 Apr
      Gain the competencies to accept, handle and process shipments containing dangerous goods according to the current edition of the IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) manual.
    Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) - Recurrent - Category 6 (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		27 Apr - 29 Apr
      Revalidate your 24-month IATA Dangerous Goods (DGR) requirement and stay up-to-date with the legal requirements, operational restrictions, and documentation regulations for dangerous goods.

    May - 2020
    Emergency Response Planning (ERP) for Civil Aviation Authorities (CAAs) and Air Navigation Service Providers (presencial, 5 das)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		4 May - 8 May
      Be prepared to plan, deliver and assure your aviation organization's Emergency Response Plan (ERP).
    Quality Management Systems (QMS) for Civil Aviation Authorities (CAA) and Air Navigation Service (ANS) Providers (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		11 May - 15 May
      Implement quality management, improve customer service and safety: for Civil Aviation Authorities and Air Navigation Service Providers.
    Fatigue Risk Management Systems (FRMS) (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		18 May - 20 May
      Benefit from the latest in scientific discoveries and developments in the management of risk posed by fatigue in aviation.

    June - 2020
    Legal and Financial Issues for Air Navigation Service Providers (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		1 Jun - 5 Jun
      Understand the impact of state rights and obligations, charging policy, facilities financing, management and control on your air navigation services.
    Cargo Sales and Key Account Management (Classroom, 4 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		2 Jun - 5 Jun
      Gain a wide range of tools and techniques to develop a strategic sales plan in a competitive air cargo industry.
    Safety Performance Indicators (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		10 Jun - 12 Jun
      Learn the foundations of Safety Performance Indicators (SPIs), and how to practically develop them, integration of safety objectives, SPIs and safety performance targets.
    Managing Aviation Policy and Regulation (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		15 Jun - 19 Jun
      Obtain the necessary skills to manage civil aviation policy and regulation.
    Aviation Law for Managers (non-lawyers) (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		22 Jun - 26 Jun
      Acquire a general understanding of aviation law relevant to air navigation services, airlines, civil aviation authorities and airports.

    July - 2020
    Cargo Business Intelligence (BI) and Competition Analysis (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		1 Jul - 3 Jul
      Learn about factors that impact performance in the industry, effective decision-making using market research and analysis to increase market share
    Management of Air Navigation Services (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		6 Jul - 10 Jul
      Gain a solid knowledge of modern ANSP business management practices that will help you guide your organization to success.
    Aviation Security Train the Trainer (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		13 Jul - 17 Jul
      Learn how to design lesson plans and instructional aids based on performance objectives. Gain practical experience by preparing and presenting short lessons during class.

    August - 2020
    Cargo Business Development for Airports (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		19 Aug - 21 Aug
      This course gives you fuel for your marketing campaigns and ideas for attracting new cargo business to your airport.
    Aircraft Recovery (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		24 Aug - 28 Aug
      Learn to manage an aircraft recovery event.

    September - 2020
    Aviation Policy and Advocacy (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		2 Sep - 4 Sep
      Learn how to use advocacy to shape current aviation policy and positively influence your organizations business objectives.
    Perishable Cargo Transportation (Classroom, 2 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		7 Sep - 8 Sep
      Gain the knowledge required to ship perishable goods safely and how to comply with the industry standards.
    Fuel Efficiency and Conservation (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		9 Sep - 11 Sep
      Want to save 3% to 5% on your fuel bill? Perform a fuel efficiency audit of your own organization and undertake a structured implementation and cultural change program.
    Shipping Lithium Batteries by Air (Classroom, 2 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		14 Sep - 15 Sep
      Acquire the skills and knowledge to pack and ship lithium batteries by air, in compliance with the ICAO Technical Instruction and IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR).
    Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) Shippers and Packers - Category 1, 2 (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		16 Sep - 18 Sep
      Knowledge and skills to pack and ship shipments containing dangerous goods as per the current edition of the IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations Manual.
    Safety Risk Management (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		23 Sep - 25 Sep
      Learn how to improve your operational safety by correctly identifying hazards, providing effective controls to mitigate safety risks, and reducing equipment loss and damage.
    Safety Management Systems (SMS) - Train the Trainer (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		28 Sep - 30 Sep
      Learn how to train to improve performance, introduce safer operations, and reduce costs in your airline while ensuring compliance with ICAO SMS requirements.

    October - 2020
    Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR) - Instructor Refresher (Classroom, 3 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		28 Oct - 30 Oct
      Stay up-to-date with your knowledge of the IATA/ICAO Dangerous Goods Regulations.

    November - 2020
    Cost Reduction Strategies for Air Navigation Service Providers (ANSPs) (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		2 Nov - 6 Nov
      Profit from the best practices of international leaders in Air Navigation Service Provision to reduce your costs and improve your operating result without compromising your safety performance.
    Performance-Based Navigation (PBN) I (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		16 Nov - 20 Nov
      Gain an overview of the history of the PBN concept, GNSS theory, Area Navigation (RNAV), RNP, and airspace planning and design.
    Performance-Based Navigation (PBN) II (Classroom, 5 days)
    Belgium, Brussels (IATA)     		23 Nov - 27 Nov
      Understand the implementation and approval processes for PBN navigation specifications from the points of view of States, Civil Aviation Authorities, Air Navigation Service Providers and aircraft operators.
