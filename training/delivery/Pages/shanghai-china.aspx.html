    IATA Training Center in Shanghai, China

    Welcome to IATA's Shanghai training venue at the Shanghai Civil Aviation College! . The college is an industry-oriented, high-level college that is committed to cultivating high-quality, high-skilled talents with a college education level training for civil aviation and social development.
