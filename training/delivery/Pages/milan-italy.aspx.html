    Milan course schedule
    December - 2019
    Aviation Security Train the Trainer (Classroom, 5 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		2 Dec - 6 Dec
      Learn how to design lesson plans and instructional aids based on performance objectives. Gain practical experience by preparing and presenting short lessons during class.
    Aviation Security Management (Classroom, 5 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		9 Dec - 13 Dec
      Assure efficient and compliant security operations with this review of industry standards, risk assessment and people management practices.

    January - 2020
    Security Audit and Quality Control (Classroom, 5 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		27 Jan - 31 Jan
      Ensure your compliance with ICAO Annex 17 audit requirements, plus international and national security programs. Learn how to conduct audits and quality control across multiple security segments.

    February - 2020
    Aviation Security Train the Trainer (Classroom, 5 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		24 Feb - 28 Feb
      Learn how to design lesson plans and instructional aids based on performance objectives. Gain practical experience by preparing and presenting short lessons during class.

    March - 2020
    Air Cargo Law and Convention (Classroom, 2 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		24 Mar - 25 Mar
      Improve your knowledge of international air cargo law conventions and recommended practices to better serve your organization and clients.
    Shipping Lithium Batteries by Air (Classroom, 2 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		26 Mar - 27 Mar
      Acquire the skills and knowledge to pack and ship lithium batteries by air, in compliance with the ICAO Technical Instruction and IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations (DGR).

    April - 2020
    Security Financial Planning and Optimization (Classroom, 3 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		27 Apr - 29 Apr
      Plan your organization’s finances to maintain compliance and high standards to address new security regulations and effectively optimize your security and protection costs.

    May - 2020
    Airline Security Operations Optimization (Classroom, 5 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		25 May - 29 May
      Review the latest industry regulations for airlines and the airport security operations that affect your business. Learn best practices to help you analyze and prevent common threats.

    June - 2020
    Cargo Claims and Complaint Handling (Classroom, 2 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		23 Jun - 24 Jun
      This course will provide you with the knowledge of the current crucial legal aspects of carriage of cargo by air for all industry stakeholders who are concerned with effective cargo claims handling and loss prevention.

    July - 2020
    ULD Train the Trainer (Classroom, 5 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		20 Jul - 24 Jul
      Enhance your skills as a Unit Load Device (ULD) instructor with the latest teaching methods.

    September - 2020
    Safety Management Systems (SMS) for Civil Aviation (Classroom, 5 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		28 Sep - 2 Oct
      This course provides the fundamentals of how to systematically manage safety in civil aviation using the Safety Management System concept developed by ICAO.

    October - 2020
    Security Management Systems - SeMS (Classroom, 5 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		26 Oct - 30 Oct
      Learn the essentials of IATA's SeMS initiative and how it can make your organization IOSA compliant. Leave the course with tools to build a SeMS that produces measurable and auditable results.

    November - 2020
    Security Risk and Crisis Management (Classroom, 5 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		23 Nov - 27 Nov
      Address security vulnerabilities in your organization and equip your facility to handle the aftermath of a crisis. Learn how to assess risks, develop a crisis plan and set up a crisis center.

    December - 2020
    Aviation Security Management (Classroom, 5 days)
    Italy, Milan (PRO-HST)     		14 Dec - 18 Dec
      Assure efficient and compliant security operations with this review of industry standards, risk assessment and people management practices.
