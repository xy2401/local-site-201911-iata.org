NDC Matchmaker


The NDC Matchmaker is a free web-based tool allowing airlines, aggregators and sellers cooperation in planning and driving NDC implementations together. Participants to the Matchmaker can share project information and communicate with each other and other partners worldwide.

Search  |  Compare  | Connect with your NDC Partners!

Watch this video to learn how to use the NDC Matchmaker.

Please note: IATA does not validate the content of the designated entities registered in the NDC Matchmaker and it does not entail any kind of certification, validation or endorsement of (i) any aspect of the entity, (ii) the quality or other aspects of its activities or products, (iii) any commercial products that may be derived from the usage of NDC messages, or (iv) compliance with NDC message schemas. All users of the NDC Matchmaker tool agree to comply with IATA’s competition law guidelines.
