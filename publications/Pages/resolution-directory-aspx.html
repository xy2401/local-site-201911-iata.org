        IATA Resolutions and Recommended Practices Directory

        This directory helps identify the different publications including the IATA Resolutions and Recommended Practices adopted by airlines at one of the six IATA Traffic Conferences.

        There are six Traffic Conferences:

        • Passenger Services, Agency and Tariffs note that most Passenger Standards are now developed under the Passenger Standards Conference 
        • Cargo Services, Agency and Tariffs

        About resolutions and recommended practices

        • Resolutions are binding for IATA member airlines. Adopting, changing or rescinding a resolution requires unanimous support from all airlines participating in the conference.
        • Recommended Practices are not binding but provide guidance. Adoption of a Recommended Practices requires the support of two thirds of the airlines participating in the conference.
