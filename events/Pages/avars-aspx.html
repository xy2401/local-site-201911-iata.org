Extended Reality or XR is the umbrella term given to virtual, augmented and mixed reality. These are no longer just buzz words, they have become reality. Our event aims to explore XR (VR, AR, MR) applications that enable airlines and aviation partners to improve operational efficiency, enhance customer experience and generate new revenue opportunities for the industry. 

Last year's Aviation Virtual and Augmented Reality Summit (AVARS) brought together 140 delegates from airlines, aviation partners, academia, technology providers and hardware manufacturers to discuss the latest trends, debate use cases and address the challenges to adoption of this technology in aviation.

Our aim for 2019 is to bring together like-minded individuals who are changing the way air transport operates and scale up Proofs-of-Concept (PoC) to implementation.

Learn from XR experts and fellow aviation geeks to develop a robust XR strategy for your organization!

New this year, Aviation XR will be co-located with the World XR this 7-9 September, 2019 in Crans Montana, Switzerland. 

The Aviation XR Hackathon and workshops will take place from 7-8 September and AVARS on 9 September.

 5-8 September will be the World XR Conference, Festival & Market.

Why you should attend

Aviation XR Weekend is the only XR event for aviation. This one of a kind event tackles various use cases of XR in aviation. Network with more than 100 organizations, including airlines, aviation partners, technology partners and hardware manufacturers, academia and research.

Keep abreast on new developments. Find out from XR experts the latest trends and solutions for the aviation industry.

Be immersed in XR experiences. Experience for yourself the latest hardware and solutions in the market.

Participate in interactive workshops / XR hackathon. Engage in interactive workshops and master classes led by XR experts. In combination with the summit, we are planning a XR hackathon in collaboration with the members of the air transport industry.

​Crans-Montana, Switzerland 

Crans-Montana - situated at 1500m on a sun-drenched plateau sitting on the Rhone Valley. Crans-Montana offers the comforts of a modern holiday resort and a vast landscape and panoramic view of the Valais Alps.


​Le Regent Congress Centre

Situated in the heart of the idyllic Swiss Alps, Le Regent is the only mountain congress center in French speaking Switzerland. It can host up to 2000 people in its largest conference room. 

 


Getting there

Accessible from Geneva or Zurich airports. Crans Montana tourism office provides a detailed description of various travel options to and from Crans Montana.

By train

From Geneva Airport: please visit the SBB train website for accurate train schedules.  From Zurich Airport: please visit the SBB train website for accurate train schedules.  

By car

Car rentals are also available in both Geneva and Zurich Airport. 

Accommodation

Crans Montana offers a number of hotels with stunning views of the alps. Please view the hotel accommodation options from the Crans Montana tourism office.
